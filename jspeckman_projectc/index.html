about

My name is Jessica Speckman and I am a graphic designer. I live, love and breath design. About a year ago I decided to go back to school and earn my BFA in Graphic Design. I chose to attend the Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD) and let me just say that it has been the best decision I could have made. I have come leeps and bounds in the one year that I have been there. I have one year left and am eager to see what is instore for me. In the time since I started at SCAD I have won four Addy Awards, two as a student and two as a professional. I am passionate at what I do and I try to let that passion shine through in my creative designs.

For about four years now I have been employeed as a Graphic Designer at a local print shop. I have had the privilage of working with many local companies and organizations on a veriety of different projects.
